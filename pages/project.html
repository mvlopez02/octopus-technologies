Factors

Security

The system must ensure that the device and the display interface are securely fastened to the cart to avoid risks during its operation. Additionally, the installation must minimize the risk of short circuits.

Comfort

The display of the data must be clear and easy to understand. It should also not obstruct the driver's visibility, and its location must be easily accessible.

Culture

The aim is for this instrument to serve an educational purpose and be used for academic exhibition.

Environment

The device must be protected against adverse weather conditions typical of the city of Barranquilla to prevent damage and ensure proper functioning.

Social

The design should focus on locally available electronic components to ensure easy acquisition and short delivery times. It is essential to securely fasten the device to the cart to prevent damage and theft.

Economy

The project aims to be economically viable and stay within the established budget.
